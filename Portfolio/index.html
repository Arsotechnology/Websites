Bio

Hello, I hope you are doing well! My name is James Karwacki and I’m currently studying Multimedia Design and Development with a concentration in Website Design and Development at DeVry University. I have been in DeVry going on three years now and expected to graduate in December, 2018. At DeVry, I have been on the Dean List 2016, 2017, and I am part of the National Society of Leadership and Success, which is a national honor society at the University. In this portfolio, I present work that I have completed at the school and also work that I have done professionally. My hope is that this portfolio represents my design and web design talents to an on looker and potential employers in a matter that is dignified. I hope one respects and appreciates the hard work that is behind all the pieces in this portfolio.

I would like to take this time to thank you for taking the time to look at my portfolio. I hope that it meets your expectations. Enjoy!
